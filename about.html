Our Mission

The web is experienced overwhelmingly on mobile devices. Yet testing how a website actually renders on a phone or tablet has remained clunky, device-dependent, or locked behind expensive developer tooling. We built MobileViewer.pro to fix that.

Our mission is simple: make mobile web testing instant, accessible, and free for everyone — from independent developers shipping their first portfolio to enterprise QA teams running multi-device test suites.

Why it matters: Over 60% of all global web traffic is mobile. Google uses mobile-first indexing. A broken mobile experience is a broken business. No team should ship without seeing the mobile version first.

Our Story

MobileViewer.pro started as an internal utility. Our founding team — a mix of frontend developers, UX designers, and QA specialists — were frustrated by the same recurring problem: client review calls where someone on the team had to quickly check a page on their phone, hold it up to a camera, and hope everyone could see it.

We built a quick internal tool to simulate mobile viewports without leaving the browser. Then we showed it to friends. Then to clients. The response was immediate — everyone needed this. In 2024, we launched MobileViewer.pro publicly.

Since launch, the tool has helped developers catch hundreds of mobile layout bugs before production, helped designers communicate responsive behaviors to stakeholders, and saved marketing teams from shipping broken landing pages to mobile audiences.

What MobileViewer.pro Offers

Our mobile viewer tool is a comprehensive responsive design tester that enables you to:

  • View any website on mobile — Enter any public URL and see it rendered at real device dimensions instantly
  • Simulate 60+ devices — Switch between iPhones, Android phones, iPads, Samsung tablets, and more without refreshing
  • Test portrait and landscape — Rotate any device to check both orientations with one click
  • Use custom screen sizes — Enter any width and height to test non-standard device dimensions
  • Capture screenshots — Document mobile previews for team reviews, bug reports, and client sign-offs
  • Share preview links — Generate shareable URLs that open with your chosen device and URL pre-loaded
  • Control zoom levels — Scale the preview to inspect fine detail or see full-page layout at a glance
